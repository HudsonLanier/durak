Trump

Trump is decided at the beginning of the game. The first card popped out of the deck decides the trump suit. A trump card can be used to break any non-trump card, or to break a lower trump card. Trump cards can only be broken by higher trump cards.

Stealing the trump card

You can replace the trump card by one of the cards in your hand. This doesn't change the trump but you get the trump card to play with as a regular card.
